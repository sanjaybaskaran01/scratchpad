Why Scratchpad

Everything you need, nothing you don't

bolt

Any key. Instant capture.

Press any character key and the scratchpad opens. No clicks, no menus — your cursor is ready. ⌘↵ to save as a clip.

content_paste

Paste anything.

Code auto-highlights, images get optimized, text files keep their name. Language is auto-detected — you can always override.

lock

Completely private.

Nothing leaves your browser. IndexedDB stores clips locally. Ephemeral clips expire in 24 h. Pin them to keep forever.

link

Share in one keystroke.

Press s to copy a URL-encoded share link. Or use P2P to transfer directly to another device — no relay server.
